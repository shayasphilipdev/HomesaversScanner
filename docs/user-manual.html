Homesavers Scanner App

User Manual

1. Sign in

  1. Open homesaversscanner.pages.dev and bookmark it.
  2. Username = your 4-digit store code (e.g. 1015).
  3. PIN = the PIN provided.
  4. Tap Sign in.
⚠️
The top-right badge shows Store Login · your store. If it shows the wrong store, sign out and sign in again before logging anything.

2. The menu

MenuWhat it's for
DashboardA summary screen. Nothing to fill in.
HO TasksReport a problem with a product (wrong price, won't scan, etc.).
Store TasksToday's checklist set by Head Office.
Product QueryAsk other stores about a product you don't recognise.
ReportsLook up past records.

3. Report a product problem (HO Tasks)

  1. Tap HO Tasks.
  2. Pick the Task Type from the dropdown.
  3. Scan or type the Product Code. The description and supplier fill in automatically if we have them.
  4. Fill the remaining fields, add a note if useful.
  5. Tap Save Record.

The task types

CodeUse it when…
A · UOM ErrorsShelf-label unit is wrong (says "each" but it's a pack).
B · Non-ScansTill can't scan the barcode. Needs 2 photos: product + barcode.
C · Wrong PricesShelf price and till price don't match.
D · Wrong DescriptionReceipt name doesn't match the product.
E · Price MarkedPrinted price on the pack differs from the till.
F · DRS ErrorsWrong deposit charge. Check the return logo is on the bottle first.
G · Promotion ErrorA promotion isn't applying at the till.
H · Stock CountOnly when HO asks for a count.
I · MiscellaneousAnything that doesn't fit the others — add a clear note.
💡
No scanner gun? Tap Use camera and point at the barcode.

4. What the statuses mean

StatusMeaningWhat you do
PendingWaiting for Head Office.Nothing — wait.
Completed by HOHO has fixed it.Check the till, then tap ✓ Clear.
ClearYou've confirmed the fix.Done — it leaves your list.
✅
Clear just hides the record from your list. It's kept safely for Head Office.

5. Daily checklist (Store Tasks)

  1. Tap Store Tasks.
  2. Tap Complete on a task card.
  3. Fill the fields marked with a red * and add any photos.
  4. Tap ✓ Mark complete.

6. Product Query

For a product with no barcode, or one nobody recognises — ask the whole chain.

  1. Tap Product Query → + Ask all stores.
  2. Take a photo and write a short note.
  3. Tap Post question.
  4. When someone answers and you're sorted, tap ✓ Close thread.

7. If the internet drops

Keep working. Records save on the device and upload when you're back online. You'll see "Saved offline — will sync."

⚠️
Don't sign out while the orange Offline · queued pill is showing — wait for it to clear.

8. Quick fixes
