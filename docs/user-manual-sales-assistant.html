Getting started
Sign in, check you're at the right shop, and you're ready to log anything.

1 · The first five minutes

Think of the Scanner app as your shop-floor notebook. When a customer says "this is the wrong price," when the till can't scan something, when a fridge needs a temperature check — that's what this app is for. Head Office sees what you log; you see what HO replied. Simple loop.

What you'll do every shift

  • Log an HO Task when a product needs HO to fix something (wrong price, no barcode, wrong description…).
  • Tick off Store Tasks assigned to your store today (fridge check, end-of-day count, etc.).
  • Close the loop on yesterday's items HO has come back on — verify the fix at the till and tap ✓ Clear.
  • Ask other stores on Product Query when you find a product nobody at your shop recognises.

The three things that are always on screen

  1. Top-right: a green dot then Store Login · <Your Store>. If that's not your shop, sign out and back in.
  2. Left menu: Dashboard · HO Tasks · Store Tasks · Product Query · Reports.
  3. Top-right corner: Sign out button. Use it only when leaving the device — not after every task.
✅
Rule of thumb The top badge is the truth. Always glance at it before saving — every record is filed under whichever store is shown there.

2 · Signing in & the top badge

How to sign in

  1. Open the app at homesaversscanner.pages.dev. Bookmark it on day one.
  2. Username = the 4-digit store code on your name-tag (e.g. 1015).
  3. PIN = the one your Store Manager gave you. Default first-time PIN is 1234 — ask them to change it on your first day.
  4. Tap Sign in.

Figure 2.1 · The sign-in screen. Same form for every staff member.

Reading the top badge

BadgeMeaning
Store Login · <Store>You're signed in as a store user, and every record will go to the store named here.
Your name on the rightThe account label — usually your store name.
⚠️
Wrong store on the badge? Sign out and back in with the right username. Don't log anything in the meantime — it would file under another shop.

Changing your PIN

You can't change your own PIN — by design. Ask your Store Manager: Admin → Employees → find your name → Reset PIN. They'll set a new one and tell you what it is.

🔒
Never share your PIN Everything you do is tagged with whoever is signed in. Sharing means HO can't tell who did what.

4 · Reporting a product problem (HO Tasks)

This is the loop:

  1. Side menu → HO Tasks.
  2. Check the top says Working at <Your Store>. If your account is set up for multiple stores, pick the right one first.
  3. Pick the Task Type from the dropdown (A–G — see the next page).
  4. Scan or type the Product Code. The description auto-fills from the master if we have it. The matched supplier appears just above the form.
  5. Fill any extra fields the form asks for (UOM, quantity, photos, etc.).
  6. Add a Note if it'll help HO ("Customer flagged at till", "Box damaged", etc.).
  7. Tap Save Record.
[ screenshot — Task A form with auto-filled supplier ]
Figure 4.1 · After scanning the code, the description and matched supplier appear automatically.

What the supplier line means

Right under the Product Code you'll see Supplier: B&M Retail Limited (or whoever the master matched). If the master is wrong, the Supplier dropdown right below lets you correct it — pick the actual supplier and that's what gets saved.

💡
Tip — the camera scanner If you don't have a scanner gun, tap 📷 Use camera. Hold the phone steady, the app will read the same code three frames in a row before it commits. Tap ✕ Stop camera when you're done.

If your connection drops

You'll see a toast: "Saved offline — will sync when you're back online." Keep working. Records send themselves once Wi-Fi or 4G is back. Don't log the same item twice.

5 · The seven daily task types at a glance

Picking the right type matters — each one routes to a different reviewer at HO and triggers a different fix. Miscellaneous (Task I) should be a last resort.

CodeNameUse it when…
AUOM ErrorsThe shelf-label unit is wrong (label says "each" but the product is a 6-pack).
BNon-ScansThe till can't scan the barcode. Beeps red, or "item not found". Two photos required: product + barcode.
CWrong PricesShelf price and till price disagree.
DWrong DescriptionThe till receipt name doesn't match the product (wrong, misspelled, foreign language).
EPrice Marked ProductsProduct packaging shows a printed price different from what the till is charging.
FDRS ErrorsDeposit Return Scheme charge is wrong. Verify the return logo is on the product first. The app calculates the deposit for you (€0.15 under 500 ml, €0.25 at 500 ml+).
GPromotion ErrorPromotion (2-for-€5, BOGOF, etc.) isn't applying at the till.
📑
Quick guide on the page At the top of HO Tasks there's a collapsible Quick guide — daily HO tasks card. Tap Show to bring up the same table while you're training. It stays collapsed by default so it doesn't clutter the page.

Two one-off types you'll rarely use

HStock CountOnly when HO has asked for an audit. Count the items on the shop floor and log the number.
IMiscellaneousAnything that genuinely doesn't fit A–H. Add a clear note describing the situation.

6 · Task statuses & closing the loop

Every HO Task you raise moves through a small set of statuses. Knowing which is which is the single most important thing in this app.

StatusWho sets itWhat you do next
PendingAuto, on saveNothing — wait for HO to review.
Completed by HOHead OfficeGo to the till/shelf, check the fix is live, then tap ✓ Clear.
No change neededHead OfficeHO reviewed and decided nothing needs changing. Read their note, then tap ✓ Clear.
ClearYouThe record is closed and disappears from your active list. (It's kept in the database for audit.)

How to close a record (the daily clearing routine)

  1. HO Tasks → tap the filter HO completed.
  2. For each row, go to the till/shelf and verify the fix is in. Run a scan if it's a price/promo fix.
  3. Tap ✓ Clear at the end of the row.
  4. Repeat until the list is empty. That's your "inbox zero" for the shift.
🚦
"Clear" is not "Delete" Clearing only hides the record from your view. HO can still find it in Reports. The data is kept for audit — there's no way to lose work by clearing.

7 · Daily Store Tasks (checklists)

Store Tasks are checklists Head Office sends to your store on a schedule — daily, weekly, or one-off. Things like fridge temperatures, opening checks, end-of-day count.

[ screenshot — Store Tasks with To-do and Done sections ]
Figure 7.1 · Today's checklist split into To do and Done today.

Completing a task

  1. Side menu → Store Tasks.
  2. Read the instructions at the top of the card (sometimes there's a safety warning).
  3. Tap Complete.
  4. Answer every required field — these have a red *. Optional fields can be skipped.
  5. Take any photos the task asks for. Aim for clear, well-lit shots.
  6. Tap ✓ Mark complete. The card moves to Done today.

The different field types you'll see

HO can build a checklist out of any combination of these blocks. You don't need to memorise them — just fill in what you see.

  • Text / Long notes — typed answers.
  • Number, Percentage, Temperature, Rating — numeric entries with units.
  • Yes / No, Multiple choice, Checkboxes — quick taps.
  • Date / Time — pickers.
  • Signature — type your name to sign off.
  • Photo / File upload — camera capture or attach a doc.
  • Auto-calculated — a total or average computed from numbers you've already typed; you don't fill it in.
📷
Photo tip Fill the frame with the subject. Bad photos waste HO time more than no photo at all. If the first shot is blurry, retake before saving.

Helping each other
When a product turns up that nobody at your store recognises, the rest of the chain is one tap away.

8 · Product Query — ask other stores

Sometimes a product turns up with no scannable barcode, an obscure brand, or a returns slip nobody can place. Product Query is a chain-wide notice board: post a photo, ask a question, any other store can answer.

[ screenshot — Product Query feed with open threads ]
Figure 8.1 · The Product Query feed. Open threads from any store, newest first.

When to use it

  • A product has no barcode and isn't in the master — and you'd rather ask than guess.
  • A customer returned something you don't recognise.
  • You're not sure who the supplier is for a one-off line.

Posting a question

  1. Side menu → Product Query.
  2. Tap + Ask all stores.
  3. Take a photo of the product. Required.
  4. Write a short note saying what you'd like to know. Keep it focused: "No barcode, anyone know the supplier?" beats "help".
  5. Tap Post question.

Answering a question (any signed-in user)

  1. Open Product Query — the open feed shows you what's been asked.
  2. Tap a thread to expand.
  3. Tap + Add answer. Type a note (required); attach a photo if it helps (optional).
  4. Tap Post answer.

Closing your own thread

When you've got the answer you need, scroll to your own thread, tap ✓ Close thread. Closed threads disappear from everyone's view. Only the asker can close — no one else can moderate or delete.

💬
Etiquette Keep photos focused. One product, one question, well-lit. The faster other stores can recognise what you're showing them, the faster you get an answer.

When things go wrong
Offline, lost records, weird error messages — the simple answers are nearly always the right ones.

9 · Working offline

The Scanner works even when Wi-Fi and 4G are both down. Your records save locally and upload themselves when the connection is back.

The signs to watch for

WhereWhat it means
🟠 Offline · N queued pill in the top navThe app can't reach the server right now. N records are waiting locally.
Toast: "Saved offline — will sync when you're back online."This specific record was held. It'll upload when you reconnect.
Toast: "Synced 3 queued records."Connection is back, those 3 just uploaded.

The one rule

🚫
Don't sign out while the orange pill is showing. Wait for the queue to clear (no pill, or zero queued) before signing out. If you sign out with queued records, they may be lost.

What to do if the pill won't clear

  1. Check Wi-Fi / 4G is actually back — open another website (e.g. google.ie) in a new tab.
  2. Tap the orange pill — it opens the Sync page where you can see exactly what's queued and retry.
  3. If a specific record keeps failing, screenshot the error and tell your Store Manager.
📡
Offline is normal Stockrooms and back corridors have patchy signal. The app is built for this — keep working and trust the queue.

10 · Reports for your own history

You won't live in Reports — but it's where you go when you need to look something up.

Two tabs

TabWhat it shows
HO recordsEvery HO Task (A–G) you've raised, with its current status. Hides Cleared by default.
Store tasksYour checklist completions with the answers you typed.

Filtering

  • From / To — date range.
  • Stores — multi-select. For most Sales Assistants this is just your own store.
  • Task types — pick any combination.
  • Status — Pending / Completed / Cleared / etc.
  • Templates (Store tasks tab) — which checklist.

Running a report

  1. Set the filters you want.
  2. Tap Run report.
  3. Use the table that appears. If a lot of rows match, you'll see "Showing 200 of 1,243 records" with a Load more button.
  4. Tap ↓ CSV if you want to download what's on screen for Excel.
🔍
Searching for a product There's no global search box — set the filters, run the report, then use your browser's Ctrl+F to find a product code or word in the table.

12 · FAQ

Can I edit a record after saving it?

No, by design. If something needs correcting, raise a new record with a note explaining the change. HO will see both and the audit trail stays intact.

What's the difference between "Completed by HO" and "Clear"?

Completed by HO means HO has actioned the issue at their end. Clear means you have verified at the till that the fix is in. The record only disappears from your list once you tap Clear.

Can I see my closed Product Query threads later?

No — once closed, threads are hidden from every user including the asker. The data is kept in the database for audit, but it's not surfaced anywhere in the UI. Only close once you're done.

Why is my UOM warning showing on an Eachs record?

Eachs means a single piece. If the product comes in a pack, every item inside must use the same UOM. Verify pack contents before saving.

I logged a record on the wrong store — what now?

Tell your Store Manager. Back-office can delete the misfiled record. Then re-log it under the correct store.

Does it work on iPhone / Android / desktop?

All three. It's a website that works in any modern browser. On iPhone/Android, "Add to Home Screen" turns it into an app-style launcher.

My scanner gun isn't typing into the Product Code field.

Tap once inside the Product Code field first to give it focus, then scan. The gun behaves like a keyboard — it needs a focused input.

Why doesn't the till price change immediately after I raise a Wrong Price task?

HO has to action it first. You'll see the record go to Completed by HO when that's done. Then verify at the till and tap Clear.

Can two people use the same login?

Don't. Every action is logged under the signed-in account; sharing makes the audit trail meaningless. Each Sales Assistant has their own username and PIN.

Is there a dark mode?

Yes — toggle the moon/sun icon at the top. This manual itself is light-mode only.

🎓
Last word Almost everything in this app is three taps. If a workflow feels like more than five, you're probably on the wrong task type — back out and pick a better-fitting one.
