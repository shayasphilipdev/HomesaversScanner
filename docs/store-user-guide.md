# Homesavers Scanner — Store Staff Guide

A short, printable guide for Sales Assistants and Store Managers.
Keep one copy near the till.

URL: **https://homesaversscanner.pages.dev**

---

## Cheat sheet (one-pager)

| Action | What to do |
|---|---|
| **Sign in** | Username = your **4-digit store code** (e.g. `1015`). PIN = the one your Manager gave you. |
| **Confirm you're in the right store** | Top-right of every screen: a green dot then **`Store Login · <Your Store Name>`**. |
| **Report a problem with a product** | Side menu → **HO Tasks** → pick the type → scan the barcode → fill the form → **Save Record**. |
| **See today's checklist** | Side menu → **Store Tasks**. Each item has a **Complete** button. |
| **Close a finished item** | When the badge says **Completed by HO**, tap **✓ Clear** to remove it from your list. |
| **Sign out** | Top-right → **Sign out** (only do this if someone else needs to log in on the same device). |

---

## 1. Signing in for the first time

1. Open the app in the browser. Bookmark the page.
2. **Username** = the 4-digit store code on your name tag, e.g. `1015` for Tallaght.
3. **PIN** = `1234` for first sign-in. **Change it immediately** — ask your Store Manager (Admin → Employees → Reset PIN).
4. The top of every screen will then show:
   - 🟢 **Store Login** — proves you're signed in as your store, not Head Office.
   - **· Your Store Name** — proves you're working at the right shop.
   - Your display name on the right.

> If the badge shows the **wrong store**, sign out and back in. Do not log records — they would go to another shop.

---

## 2. The four screens you'll use

### **Dashboard**
A summary of what's been recorded recently. You can't do much here — it's just a status board. The big "Total records" tile is for the day's run-rate; the activity chart shows the last 14 days.

### **HO Tasks**
Where you report a **problem with a product** (wrong price, can't scan, wrong description, etc.).
**You must pick the store at the top before recording anything.**

### **Store Tasks**
Your **daily / weekly checklist** assigned by Head Office (e.g. fridge temperature check, end-cap photo). Items appear automatically when due. Tap one, complete it, photo if needed, tap **Mark complete**.

### **Reports**
Read-only look at past records. Useful when a Manager asks "did we log that one yesterday?"

## 3. How to report a product problem (HO Tasks)

> Use this whenever a customer or you spots that something is wrong with a product at the shelf or at the till.

### 3.1 — Which task type do I use?

Pick from the **Task Type** dropdown:

| Code | Use it when… |
|---|---|
| **A — UOM Errors** | The unit of measure on the shelf label is wrong (e.g. label says "each" but it's actually a 6-pack). |
| **B — Non-Scans** | The till **can't scan** the barcode — it beeps red or shows "item not found." Requires a photo of the product and a photo of the barcode. |
| **C — Wrong Prices** | The shelf price and the till price disagree. |
| **D — Wrong Description** | The till receipt name doesn't match what's on the product (or is spelled wrong / in a foreign language). |
| **E — Price Marked Products** | The product has a **printed price on the packaging** that's different from what the till is charging (common with imported stock). |
| **F — DRS Errors** | The Deposit Return Scheme charge is wrong. **Always check for the return logo on the bottle first** — only DRS-eligible products carry one. |
| **G — Promotion Error** | The promotion (2-for-€5, BOGOF, etc.) isn't applying at the till. |
| **H — Stock Count** | Counting product on the shop floor for a one-off audit. |
| **I — Miscellaneous** | Anything else worth flagging that doesn't fit above. |

### 3.2 — Filling the form (general flow)

1. Side menu → **HO Tasks**.
2. **Current Store** is at the top. If you have only one store, it's already set. If you have several, pick the right one **before scanning**.
3. **Task Type**: pick from the dropdown.
4. **Product Code / Barcode**: scan with the gun, or type the barcode. Wait a second for it to look up the description — if the product is on the master, the description fills in automatically.
5. Fill any extra fields the form asks for:
   - **B (Non-Scans)**: take two photos — one of the **product** (label visible) and one of the **barcode**. Both are required.
   - **F (DRS)**: pick the bottle **size** and how many are in the unit. The app calculates the deposit for you (e.g. *6 × 25c = €1.50*).
   - **G (Promotion)**: describe the promo ("2 for €5") and the price the till charged.
   - **H (Stock Count)**: just the count.
6. Add a **Note** if helpful — e.g. *"Customer Mary flagged this on the till"*.
7. Tap **Save Record**.

> Tip: If the page says **"Saved offline — will sync when you're back online"**, that's fine. It will upload itself once Wi-Fi or 4G comes back. Don't enter it twice.

### 3.3 — After you save

The record appears in the list below the form with a **Pending** badge. Head Office reviews it. You'll see it change to one of:

- 🟢 **Completed by HO** — HO fixed it (price change, master update, etc.).
- ⚪ **No change needed** — HO investigated and decided no change is needed. A note from HO may explain why.

When you see either, check the till / shelf to make sure the fix is live, then tap **✓ Clear** to remove the row from your active list. The record is kept in the database — it just disappears from your view.

## 4. Daily Store Tasks (checklists)

Side menu → **Store Tasks**.

You'll see a *"Today's tasks"* page broken into **To do** and **Done today**.

For each task:
1. Tap **Complete** on the card.
2. Fill anything the task asks for (photo, notes, a yes/no answer, etc.). Required fields have a red asterisk.
3. Tap **✓ Mark complete**.

The card moves into the **Done today** section with a green tick.

If the page is empty, nothing was assigned today — that's normal on quiet days.

> Head Office decides which tasks appear in which stores and on which days. If you think something is missing, mention it to your Store Manager — they'll raise it with HO.

---

## 5. Working offline

You can keep logging records when the internet is down (e.g. the Wi-Fi router restarted).

- Saved records show **"Saved offline — will sync when you're back online"**.
- The app sends them automatically the moment connectivity comes back. You don't have to do anything.
- A small **offline indicator** appears at the top while you're offline.

**Don't sign out while offline** — the queued records may be lost. Wait until you're back online and see the records in the list before logging out.

---

## 6. Switching stores (only relevant if you cover multiple shops)

If your account is set up for more than one shop:

1. Top-right context badge shows your **current store**.
2. On any **HO Tasks** page, the **Current Store** dropdown at the top lets you switch.
3. Always check the badge after switching — every record you log goes to the store shown there.

If your badge shows the wrong store and you can't switch from the dropdown, ask the Admin to add your other store under Admin → Employees → Edit → Store / Area scope.

---

## 7. Resetting / changing your PIN

You can't change your own PIN. Ask the **Store Manager** or **Admin**:

- Admin → Employees → find your name → **Reset PIN** → set the new one.

If you're locked out, tell your Manager — they can do it without contacting HQ.

## 8. Quick troubleshooting

| What you see | Likely cause | What to do |
|---|---|---|
| "Username or PIN doesn't match" | Wrong code or PIN | Try again. Check Caps Lock. Ask Manager for a reset. |
| Top badge shows **Head Office Login** | You signed in as an HO/admin account | Sign out and use your store account. |
| Top badge shows the **wrong store** | Account scoped to another shop | Sign out, sign back in with the right username. |
| Mandatory "Current Store" picker appears red | You haven't picked a store yet | Pick your shop from the dropdown before scanning. |
| Form complains "Supplier required" / "Product photo required" | Mandatory field missing | Scroll up — the missing field will have a red note. |
| "Saved offline" appears on every save | Wi-Fi / 4G is down | Keep working — it will sync when you're back online. |
| Records page shows nothing | Wrong filter selected | Tap **All** at the top to reset. |
| Same record keeps appearing as Completed | You haven't tapped **✓ Clear** yet | Tap it once HO has reviewed. |

For anything else, screenshot the error and send it to your Store Manager or to support.

---

## 9. Things to never do

- **Don't share your PIN.** Every action is logged under whoever's signed in.
- **Don't log the same product twice in a row** unless the situation is genuinely different — duplicates make HO's job harder.
- **Don't sign out while you have unsynced (offline) records.** Wait until the green sync indicator disappears.
- **Don't use a personal phone with a saved PIN** unless your Manager has approved that device.
