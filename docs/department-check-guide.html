Department Check — User Guide

Scanning  ·  Reports  ·  Clearing records

1
How to do a department check
👥  Store staff
  1. 1
    Log in to the Homesavers Scanner app using your store PIN.
  2. 2
    Tap HO Tasks, then select Department Check from the task type list.
📷
HO Tasks & Department Check selection
Save as screenshots/step-ho-tasks.png
  1. 3
    Scan the product barcode using the scanner gun or phone camera. Alternatively, type the barcode into the Product code field and press Enter.
  2. 4
    If the product is found in the system, its details will be pulled in automatically — no manual entry needed.
  3. 5
    Tap Save to submit the record, then scan the next barcode to move on to the next product.
⚠
Always scan the barcode on the product itself — do not scan the Product ID printed on the shelf edge label (SEL).
ℹ
If a product is not available or the device is offline, the product details fields won't populate. Keep scanning and save each record as usual. Records will be updated in the system when the device is back online. Do not exit the app.
2
How to pull the report
👥  Store staff
💻
This report can be run from the store back-office PC or from your phone. Log in using your store PIN, then follow the steps below.
  1. 1
    Go to Reports in the top navigation.
  2. 2
    Set the Date range — choose From and To dates for the period you want to review.
  3. 3
    In the Task type filter, select Department Check, then click Run Report.
  4. 4
    The table updates with all matching records. Columns shown: Task type, Store, Product ID, Department, Product Barcode, Status, Date.
  5. 5
    To download: click Export CSV at the top right of the table and open in Excel.

3
How to clear records in bulk
👥  Store staff

Once back-office has reviewed and completed your records, clear them regularly to keep your list tidy and prevent a large backlog. This can be done from the back-office PC, a Pricer, or your phone.

  1. 1
    Go to HO Tasks — your submitted records appear in the list below the scanning area.
  2. 2
    A Select all button appears at the top of the list when there are records ready to clear. Tap it to select all, or tick individual checkboxes.
  3. 3
    Once records are selected, tap Clear selected to archive them.
  4. 4
    The cleared records are removed from the active list.
